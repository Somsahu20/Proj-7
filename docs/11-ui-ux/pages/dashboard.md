## Components

### Balance Card
- Total owed to others
- Total owed by others
- Net balance (color coded)
- Settle up button

### Group Cards
- Group icon/image
- Group name
- User's balance in group
- Member count
- Click to open group

### Activity Feed
- Recent activities across all groups
- Infinite scroll
- Click to go to relevant page

### Quick Actions
- Floating action buttons
- Most common actions
